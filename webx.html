Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Jetweb offer? ▼
Jetweb offers a wide range of web development services including custom website development, UI/UX design, e-commerce integration, web app development, SEO optimization, and ongoing maintenance and support.
How long does it take to complete a project? ▼
Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity of the project. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take several months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.
Do you offer post-launch support? ▼
Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support and maintenance packages to ensure your website continues to perform optimally. This includes regular updates, security patches, and technical support.
